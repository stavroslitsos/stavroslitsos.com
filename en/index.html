Who is Stavros Litsos?

Lead manual therapist with specialist competence in clinical orthopaedic medicine and ultrasound diagnostics.

Stavros is a manual therapist educated at the University of Bergen and holds a master’s degree in sports physiology and biomechanics from the Norwegian School of Sport Sciences. He also has specialist competence in clinical orthopaedic medicine (OMI Cyriax) and further training in ultrasound diagnostics and injection treatment from Norway and England.

He has extensive experience in elite sport and sports medicine, with a focus on rapid rehabilitation and returning athletes to competition after injury, and follows up a number of teams and athletes at national-team level. He is also a supervisor for master’s students in clinical manual therapy at the University of Bergen.
