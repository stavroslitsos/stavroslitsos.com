Medication and corticosteroid injections

If medication and corticosteroid injections are required, this is discussed and administered on an interdisciplinary basis and through close collaboration with your GP or an orthopaedic / physical medicine specialist or referring medical practitioner. The assessment is carried out by the undersigned together with a physician, where there is agreement on the condition and the indication for injection. Treatment can then be initiated.
